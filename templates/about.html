About this project - Talent Beyond Boundaries


Supporting labor migration by matching refugee skills to open positions

This proposal looks at how organizations can drive 'Diversity and Inclusion' programs by using a data driven approach to match refugee talent with current opportunities for employement. Such an approach has the potential to address business needs to hire highly skilled, dedicated professionals while also having a range of social benefits that support UN development initiatives. (Cousins & Laurie 2019)

How it would work:

The data sources - on the talent supply side, data may come from specially curated databases such as the 'Talent Beyond Boundaries' talent catalog, or via available social media feeds. Talent demand would be referenced via the Thinknum NYSE Jobs Database, and the matching engine would use a NLP approach such as the 'OpenSkills Project' from Data at work.
