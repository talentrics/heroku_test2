About Talentrics - Workforce Science Made Simple

Often referred to as ‘People Analytics’, data science can help everyday HR professionals drive workforce strategy, deliver on agile leadership, and improve employee engagement.Traditionally, the HR function served a more qualitative function in the organization. People entered the profession because they had a passion for 'working with people'. This passion for people is still true today, but there is a new set of skills that HR practitioners need to have so they can influence their organization: people analytics, agile leadership, and user experience.

As a Human Capital Consultant with an interest in People Analytics, I have built a resource for HR professionals like myself. I call it 'Talentrics' It is a place for like minded professionals to share knowledge and grow as the industry does. You can visit the site here: https://www.talentrics.com
